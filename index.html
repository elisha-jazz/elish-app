Worship Ministry

The Victory City Church Mutungo worship ministry is a vibrant ministry that is highly developing from one level to another. The ministry has grown from talented musicians and singers to highly devoted and committed ministers who are highly recognising the importance of creating an atmosphere that fascilitates people to the throne of God.

Men's Ministry

The Men's ministry is made up of men who love the Lord including married men who are highly involved in their families. Together with their Pastor (Pastor Trust Nongo), they grow and develop together in the love of Christ. They grow into men of purpose that have values like integrity, honesty, love, care and many more.

Women's Ministry

Here women are developed and groomed to the women that are exemplary in their communities and families. Here the Pastor walks along with every woman in their journey as they follow Christ.

Youth Ministry

There has been fire and revival that has been re-kindled among the youth in VCC Mutungo. These youth are now vibrant and seriously participating in the ministries in church including the worship ministry, the asher's ministry,the visitor's care ministry as well as other church ministries.

Children's Ministry

Together with marvalous teachers, the children are surrounded with God fearing friends and a growth community that enables them to grow and develop into children that can be exemplary in their communities as well as schools.
